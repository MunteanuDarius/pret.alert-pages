Privacy Policy

How we handle your information when you track products and receive price-change notifications.

Effective Date: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Price Alert (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Android application. Price Alert allows users to track products and receive notifications when prices change.

By using Price Alert, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

Information We Collect

Price Alert may collect and store the following types of information:

  • Tracked items — product URLs, names, prices, and related tracking data you choose to save in the app.
  • User preferences — settings such as notification preferences, check intervals, and display options.
  • Usage and diagnostic data — anonymized or aggregated analytics and crash reports collected through third-party services (see Third-Party Services below).
  • Device information — basic device and app version data that may be collected by Google Play Services and Firebase services to support app functionality and diagnostics.

Price Alert does not collect payment information and does not require users to create an account.

How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Store and display your tracked products and price history on your device.
  • Send notifications you have requested when tracked prices change.
  • Apply your preferences and settings within the app.
  • Monitor app performance, diagnose crashes, and improve stability and features.
  • Understand general usage patterns to improve the user experience.
We do not sell your personal information. Price Alert does not share or sell personal data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Price Alert may use the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase Analytics — helps us understand how the app is used through aggregated, anonymized usage data. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash reports and diagnostic information to help us fix bugs and improve reliability. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — provides core Android platform services required for app distribution and operation. Google Privacy Policy

These services may process data on servers located outside your country. We encourage you to review the privacy policies linked above for more details on how those providers handle data.

Data Retention

Price Alert does not operate its own backend servers and does not transmit tracked product data to developer-controlled servers. Tracked products, alerts, and preferences are stored locally on the user’s device.

Tracked items and user preferences are stored locally on your device for as long as you keep the app installed and the data in the app. Analytics and crash data retained by Firebase and Google are subject to those providers’ retention policies.

You can uninstall the app at any time to remove locally stored data from your device. Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete data already collected by third-party analytics or crash reporting services prior to removal.

User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

  • Access or review data stored locally in the app on your device.
  • Delete tracked items and reset preferences within the app.
  • Disable notifications through your device or in-app settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored data.
  • Contact us with questions or requests related to this policy.

To exercise these rights or ask questions about your data, please use the contact information below.

Children’s Privacy

Price Alert is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to address the request.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app, our practices, or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
